ABOUT

Kian Beydemüller is an emerging actor and model who entered the industry in 2024. Born in Uganda and raised in both Germany and the UK, Kian has been immersed in diverse cultural influences throughout his life. His passion for acting and modeling was evident from a young age, leading to his first official modeling shoot in London at the age of 11.

Kian remains actively engaged in the industry, continually expanding his knowledge and experience. Most recently, he appeared in Win The Crowd Films' Hospital Drama (2024) as Maxwell, a hospital visitor. Currently, Kian is seeking representation and is not yet signed with an agent or manager.
